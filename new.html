Investor Q&A

What does your company do?

Khal.com is a place where cooking enthusiasts can show the world their cooking skills. It is a social network and professional network for people who like cooking.


Where do you want to be in 5 years?

To be the "Go To" place for anyone who loves to cook professionally or for enjoyment


Why did you choose this idea? Why do you care?

My dad loves to cook. I saw all the different things he cooked and how beautifully they turned out. He loved to have people over for dinner. They always complimented his cooking. I love to cook too and to invite people over. None of us are professional chefs. Many people love to show their cooking skills We give them a platform to do so.


How far along are you? What's your biggest obstacle?

The web app Khal.com is ready and launched with users and some very interesting recipes. Our biggest obstacle is to rope someone famous to the platform.


Who competes with you? What do you understand that they don't?

Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, twitter and other social networks are our biggest competitors.
They are not designed only for cooking. A chef cannot submit his Instagram profile for a job application. Khal will be exclusively about cooking


How will you make money?

We have 2 plans to monetize

  1. Advertising
  2. Tasting: Post covid-19. We will start a tasting feature where users can sell tastings of their recipes

What are the biggest risks? If you fail, what would be the reason? What has to go right for you to succeed?

I have started a company and taken it from 0 to $16 million in annual sales in around 4 years selling in a very competitive market. I am very hardworking and will always find a work around to make things work. Eventually we might have competition. However they will need at least a year to catch on. We have the advantage of being 1st to Market. We have the need to grow big fast
